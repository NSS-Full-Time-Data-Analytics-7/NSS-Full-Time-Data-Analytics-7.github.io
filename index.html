NSS Staff and Alumni

Often in society, it becomes almost natural for one to expect to become just another cog in the machine. It is much harder to go into a situation and expect to be seen and uplifted. Through every step of this journey, NSS has broken this stigma. By cultivating a strong community where acceptance and understanding are continuously pushed, a new era of tech professionals has been birthed. No matter who we called, emailed, slacked, or sent a carrier pigeon out to, there was always a warm response returned. It is an understatement to say the individuals that came before us “paved” the way to greatness. Simply because they are still to this day actively paving the road to success for not only us, themselves, and our community but for future generations. NSS has built a strong foundation that only continues to get better over time because of the staff members that give us their all and our alumni that never forget their roots.
